#  Day 4 Advanced Nmap & Enumeration

## What I Practiced Today

Today focused on advanced network reconnaissance using Nmap. The goal was to understand how systems expose services, ports, and operating system details through scanning techniques.

---

##  Commands Used

###  Basic Localhost Scan

- nmap localhost

### Service detection scan

- sudo nmap -sV localhost

### Operation service detection scan

- sudo nmap -O localhost

### Enumeration scan

- sudo nmap -A localhost
