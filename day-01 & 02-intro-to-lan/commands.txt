## Day 1 & 2 -Into to Local Area Network(LAN)

### Commands Used

### Network Configuration
**Command: `ipconfig`  
**Outcome: Retrieved local IP address, subnet mask, and default gateway.

 Evidence:  
screenshots/day-01/ipconfig.png

### Connectivity Testing
**Command:** `ping google.com`  
**Outcome:** Verified connectivity to external servers and measured response time.

 Evidence:  
screenshots/day-01/ping.png  

---

###  DNS Resolution
**Command:** `nslookup google.com`  
**Outcome:** Resolved domain name into its corresponding IP address.

 Evidence:  
screenshots/day-01/nslookup.png  

---

### Network Path Analysis
**Command:** `tracert google.com`  
**Outcome:** Identified the route taken by packets through multiple network hops.
