#  Cybersecurity Learning Journey

##  Day 1 — Introduction to LAN

---

##  Objective
To understand and develop the fundamentals of Local Area Networks (LAN) and gain practical experience with essential networking commands used in cybersecurity.

---

##  Key Concepts Learned
- Structure and purpose of a LAN
- Role of IP addresses in device identification
- DNS and domain name resolution
- Function of routers and default gateways
- Basics of packet based communication across networks

---

##  Tools & Commands Used
- Command Prompt (Windows)
- Networking utilities:
  - `ipconfig`
  - `ping`
  - `nslookup`
  - `tracert`

---

## Challenges Encountered
- Initial difficulty understanding abstract networking concepts
- Lack of access to premium TryHackMe content
- Addressed through independent command-line practice and research

---

##  Learning Outcomes
- Gained foundational understanding of LAN fundamentals
- Developed familiarity with basic networking commands
- Improved understanding of how internet communication works at a high level
